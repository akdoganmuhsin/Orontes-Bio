My Story

I’m a passionate triathlete and biologist born and raised in the vibrant landscapes of Antakya, where the Orontes River (Asi Nehri) flows through the heart of my story. Growing up near the river, I spent my days cycling through its scenic surroundings and swimming 1km daily, a routine that fuels my discipline and love for nature. My connection to this region runs deep, rooted in its rich history and natural beauty.

Education

My journey began in Gümüşgöze, where I attended Gümüşgöze Primary School, a place steeped in history near the renowned Yakto Mosaics and ancient Roman castle ruins, once a summer retreat for emperors. This unique blend of heritage and nature sparked my curiosity for the world around me. I continued my education at Necmi Asfuroğlu Anadolu Lisesi, Antakya’s premier public high school, where I developed a strong foundation for my academic pursuits.

I pursued my bachelor’s degree in biology at ODTÜ, where I honed my scientific expertise and passion for discovery. Now, as a master’s student at ODTÜ, I’m diving deeper into biotechnology, exploring innovative solutions to real-world challenges.

Orontes Bio

With a deep-rooted passion for biology and a drive to innovate, I founded Orontes Bio to bridge science and the cultural legacy of my hometown. My experiences as a triathlete—balancing endurance, strategy, and resilience—shape my approach to tackling challenges in biotechnology.

Passion for Triathlon

Whether I’m swimming my daily 1km, cycling through Antakya’s hills, or pushing the boundaries of scientific research, I’m driven by a commitment to growth, sustainability, and making a meaningful impact.
